Master Settings
Master String

The Master String (password) is what will be used to encrypt your data. This means that it can't change. You also MUST remember it, otherwise you will not be able to decrypt your data correctly.

PIN

Your PIN will be used for you to be able to unlock the app, within the same session.

Remember: closing this tab/window will invalidate your session, and will require you to re-enter your Master String and PIN once again.
